Coding, also known as programming, is the art and science of instructing a computer to perform specific tasks. It involves writing sets of instructions in a programming language that a computer can understand and execute. Coding is the foundation of software development, and it plays a crucial role in building everything from simple web applications to complex artificial intelligence systems.

One of the key aspects of coding is problem-solving. Programmers often encounter challenges and must devise creative solutions to overcome them. This problem-solving aspect is what makes coding both challenging and rewarding. It requires logical thinking, attention to detail, and the ability to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps.

Coding languages come in various flavors, each designed for specific purposes. Popular programming languages include Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, and Ruby, among others.
